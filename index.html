About Buffalo Strings

Learn more about our group and what we offer

Buffalo Strings is an ensemble of professional musicians who also perform with the Buffalo Philharmonic Orchestra. Buffalo Strings musicians perform high quality music and have a wealth of experiences and successes in providing live music for different types of events. Buffalo Strings has a large repertoire list including classical music, church music, popular songs, standards, movie scores, and jazz themes. Buffalo Strings is the perfect complement to your special celebrations and events.

The group has played numerous engagements over the years, in venues covering the Western NY region and is available in a variety of forms, depending on the sound you'd like, the size of your venue, and your price range.
