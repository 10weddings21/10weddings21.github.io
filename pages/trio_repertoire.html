About Custom Repertoire

If you do not find the popular song you are interested in, we would still be glad to perform it if you provide the sheet music. If not, for a small fee we can arrange it, if the songs allows.

Trio Reperoitre Selections

The Trio repertoire list includes a variety of styles. We hope you will be able to find a style that will fit your event the best. Our classical selections will bring a traditional and unique spirit to your event. If you would like a more modern or contemporary spirit, we hope you will find the pieces you are looking for in our Popular songs, Movie Music, or Jazz standards lists.
